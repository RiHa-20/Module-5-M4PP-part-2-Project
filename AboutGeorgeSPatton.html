The early life of George S. Patton

George S. Patton was born in 1885 in San Gabriel,California to George S. Patton II, an attorney, and Ruth Wilson. Ruth was the daughter if Benjamin Davis Wilson, the second mayor of Los Angeles and an Ranchero.Patton's family had already had a long list of members that where in several wars which inspired him to become a war hero himself. In 1910, Patton married Beatrice Ayer, a childhood friend, they had three children: Beatrice, Ruth, and George IV who also became a major general.
